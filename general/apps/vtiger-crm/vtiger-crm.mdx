

<Info>
    This page explains the steps you need to follow to set up the Vtiger CRM connection in PreCallAI. If any of the steps are outdated, please let us know!
</Info>

1. Go to the [link](https://www.vtiger.com/) and create an account.
2. Go to **My Preferences** of your account.
3. Copy **Access Key** value from Vtiger CRM to PreCallAI.
4. Fill **Username** field as your Vtiger CRM account email on PreCallAI.
5. Fill **Domain** field as if your dashboard url is `https://acmeco.od1.vtiger.com`, paste `acmeco.od1` to the field on PreCallAI.
6. Click **Submit** button on PreCallAI.
7. Congrats! Vtiger CRM connection is created.

## Affiliates

1. Commission Rate: 5% per referral

2. Referral Process:
   Send an email invitation with your referral link to your friends.
   Your friends subscribe to a paid plan using your referral link.
   You start earning recurring commissions from your first purchase.

3. Withdrawal:
   Fill out your bank information.
   The minimum withdrawal amount is $10.

You will get all the withdrawal request details [here](https://app.qcall.ai/admin/affiliate).

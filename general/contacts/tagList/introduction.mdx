## What is a <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Tag List</span> ?

We essentially group a bunch of tags and form a contact list. Contact list is just a group of users that we intend to target.

## How to add a <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Tag List</span> ?

Follow these steps to add a tag list:<br/>

Login into the [dashboard](https://app.qcall.ai/admin/dashboard) and open [Tag List](https://app.qcall.ai/admin/tags) section.<br/>
Then:

<span style={{ fontWeight: 800 }}>Step 1 :</span> Cick on the first 'Tags' button.

<span style={{ fontWeight: 800 }}>Step 2 :</span> Add Name and description <br />

<span style={{ fontWeight: 800 }}>Step 3 :</span> Cick on 'Save' button

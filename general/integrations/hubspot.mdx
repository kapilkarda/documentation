## Here are the steps to connect your QCall AI account with Hubspot.

1. Log in to the QCall AI dashboard.

2. Click on the QCall AI logo in the top left corner to open the menu.

3. Select "Integrations" from the menu.

4. In the Integrations section, you will see HubSpot.

5. Follow the normal process to create a new HubSpot account or sign in to your existing, HubSpot account.

6. Complete the steps to connect your QCall AI account with HubSpot.

Once you have completed these steps, your QCall AI account will be integrated with HubSpot, allowing you to sync data.

Now, here is the instruction that helps you to check the contact data at CRM in HubSpot

1. Create or sign in at HubSpot.

2. Open the HubSpot Dashboard.

3. Click on the Side left menu.

4. Find the CRM option and click on it.

5. You will see different fields, select Contacts.

6. You will see all the synced contacts there.
